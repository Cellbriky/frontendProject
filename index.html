Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic Omelette combine beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vagetable, or meats.

Prepartion time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vetegables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper untill they are well mixed. you can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a ffullfier texture
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cooking the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling. pour in the egg. Hit the pan to eansure the egg hit the surface
  4. Adding filling(Optional): When the egg begin to set at the edge but are still sighly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over on half of the omelette
  5. Fold serve: As the omelette continue to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
